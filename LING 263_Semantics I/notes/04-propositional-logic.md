# Propositional Logic

## Notation
- Propositions (atoms): $p$, $q$, $r$
- Logical connectives: 
  - Negation: $\neg$
  - Conjunction: $\land$
  - Disjunction: $\lor$
  - Implication: $\implies$
  - Biconditional: $\iff$
- The interpretation of $\phi$: $[\phi]^I$

## Terminology
- **Possible world**: a world in which each proposition has a value, i.e. one row in a truth table
